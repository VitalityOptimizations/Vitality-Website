Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between plans?
Lifetime gives you permanent access to the current version of Vitality. Lifetime Updates includes all future updates and new features at no additional cost — the best long-term value.
Can I upgrade from Lifetime to Lifetime Updates later?
Yes, you can upgrade by paying the difference. Open a support ticket on our Discord and our team will assist you with the upgrade process.
How do I activate my license?
After purchase, join our Discord server and create a support ticket. Our team will verify your purchase and provide your license key with full activation instructions.
Is the BIOS Tweaker a separate product?
Yes, BIOS Tweaker is a standalone tool for editing NVRAM and BIOS settings at the hardware level. It works alongside Vitality Optimization but is purchased separately.
